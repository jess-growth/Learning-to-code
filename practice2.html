Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Dragon's Breath Pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of Unicorn Tears
  • 3 slices of Phoenix Feather
  • 4 ounces of Mermaid Scales
  • 5 drops of Fairy Dust
  • 6 grams of Elven Herbs
  • 7 pieces of Goblin Fruit

Instructions

  1. Preheat your cauldron to a medium-high flame.
  2. Add the Dragon's Breath Pepper and stir until it starts to sizzle.
  3. Pour in the Unicorn Tears and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Gently fold in the Phoenix Feather slices, ensuring they don't burn.
  5. Mix in the Mermaid Scales and cook until they dissolve completely.
  6. Add the Fairy Dust drop by drop, stirring continuously.
  7. Sprinkle in the Elven Herbs and let the mixture thicken.
  8. Finally, toss in the Goblin Fruit and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your magical dish!
